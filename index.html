Introduction

Skip to Do'sSkip to Don'ts

I have seen way too many Minehut servers (including popular ones) make so many mistakes that cause them to be inactive, have low player retention, or just be really rushed and low quality overall. In this list, it sort of tells a lesson on what to do to actually make a good server. Nowadays, Minehut has gotten more and more low-quality servers over the years and it's time to fix that.

Don'ts

I am going to start out with this section, because it is the most important, since almost everything in this list is not followed by in most MH servers

  • DON'T make a Box server
  • DON'T make a Gens server
  • DON'T make a Anarchy server
  • DON'T make your server repetitive
  • DON'T make your server P2W
  • DON'T use MH server templates
  • DON'T use a paid plan on MH
  • DON'T rig your server's crates
  • DON'T hire random people as staff, mods or admins
  • DON'T share your account or let anyone access your server console.
  • DON'T have spammy ads on MH's Discord server.
  • DON'T DM random people on MH's Discord Server
  • DON'T punish people for silly reasons.

Do's

This is what I reccommend to do to have a great server!

  • DO expand outside of Minehut
  • DO use the External server plan (when it actually becomes easy to use)
  • DO make your server free to play
  • Play your cards right
  • DO have unique gamemodes
